## About Me

I am **Junteng Liu**, a first-year PhD candidate at the **HKUST NLP Group** (2024-Present), advised by **Professor Junxian He**. My research interests include **LLM Reasoning and RL**, **Hallucination in VLMs**, and **LLM truthfulness and Interpretability**.

## Academic Background

I graduated from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree (2020-2024). During my undergraduate studies, I was awarded the **Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship at SJTU**.
